Cornelia Chow

is an incoming Creative Developer at Squarespace in New York City.

She recently graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Finishing off her degree with a Senior Design Capstone project that aimed to replace hired cameramen at small social functionings, her team received recognition from Apple and showcased their work to engineers at Apple Headquarters in Cupertino.

In the Fall of 2018, she studied abroad at UCL in London.

Last summer, she interned at Squarespace, creating pages on the Front Site featuring customers like Keanu Reeves and Danny Bowien as well as the site's 404 page.

Previously, she has interned at GE Transportation in Melbourne, FL. as well as attended a Software Engineering Summit with Capital One in Arlington, VA.

In her free time, she enjoys drawing, lettering, watching and re-watching The Grand Budapest Hotel, and eating good food.
